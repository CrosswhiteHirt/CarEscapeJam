Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Car Escape Jam handles information when you use the game. Car Escape Jam is a puzzle game for iOS 13 and later, developed with SpriteKit and Objective-C.

Information Stored on Your Device

The game stores gameplay progress and preferences locally on your device. This may include unlocked levels, star ratings, last played level, music settings, and sound settings. This information is used only to keep your progress and preferences between sessions.

Advertising and Rewarded Ads

Car Escape Jam may show advertising, including rewarded ads. Advertising partners may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate device information, ad interaction data, and diagnostic data to deliver ads, measure performance, prevent fraud, and improve reliability.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS versions that require App Tracking Transparency permission, the app may ask for permission before accessing the advertising identifier. You can manage this permission at any time in the iOS Settings app.

Third-Party Services

The app may include third-party SDKs for advertising, attribution, analytics, or ad delivery. These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies. Third-party services used by the project may include ad network and measurement providers such as Chartboost, Vungle, InMobi, Yandex Mobile Ads, and AppMetrica-related components.

Children's Privacy

Car Escape Jam is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through a third-party service connected to the app, please contact the relevant service provider or the app publisher through the App Store support channel.

Data Sharing

The game does not directly sell personal information. Third-party advertising and measurement providers may receive information necessary to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and operate their services.

Data Retention

Local gameplay progress remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's stored data. Third-party service providers may retain data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • You can disable music and sound effects in the game settings.
  • You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete local progress by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes, when third-party SDKs change, or when legal requirements change. The updated date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the app publisher through the App Store support channel for Car Escape Jam.
