Car Escape Jam

Clear the traffic puzzle. Plan each tap, avoid roadblocks, and guide every car safely out.

A compact car escape puzzle

Car Escape Jam is a SpriteKit puzzle game built with Objective-C. Each level asks you to read the board, choose the right order, and send cars out before they collide with other cars or roadblocks.

The game supports iOS 13 and later across compatible iPhone and iPad devices. It is designed for portrait play, quick sessions, and a steady difficulty curve.

30 deterministic levels
Star ratings and saved progress
One mistake chance per level
Roadblocks in later puzzles
Music and sound controls
Rewarded revive option
