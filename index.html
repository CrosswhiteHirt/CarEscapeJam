Car Escape Jam

Clear the traffic puzzle by guiding every car out in the right order.

About the Game

Car Escape Jam is a clean and satisfying puzzle game built with SpriteKit and Objective-C. Each level places colorful cars on a compact board. Tap a car to send it forward, avoid blocked paths, and find the correct sequence to clear the board.

The game is designed for quick play sessions, steady challenge, and readable portrait gameplay across iPhone and iPad devices that support iOS 13 or later.

Gameplay

Every tap matters. A car can leave the board only when its path is clear. If another car or a roadblock stands in the way, the move fails. Players get one mistake chance before the level is lost, encouraging careful planning without slowing the game down.

  • Tap cars to move them in their facing direction.
  • Clear all cars to complete the level.
  • Replay levels to improve star ratings.
  • Use level selection to return to completed puzzles.

Technical Information

Car Escape Jam is developed with SpriteKit and Objective-C in Xcode 26.4. The app supports iOS 13 and later, uses portrait gameplay, and is adapted for all compatible iPhone and iPad screen sizes.
